Crypto prop firms are growing fast. More traders want to trade crypto with firm capital instead of risking their own. But this space has more scams than the traditional prop firm industry.

This guide covers the ones that actually pay.

---

### What Makes Crypto Prop Firms Different

Crypto prop firms usually offer:

- Crypto as the base currency (BTC, ETH, USDT)
- Crypto pairs or perpetual swaps
- Higher leverage options
- Less regulation (which cuts both ways)

The lack of regulation means you need to be more careful. There are firms that take your evaluation fee and vanish. Stick with the established names.

### What To Watch For

1. Crypto-only prop firms that have no track record. If they launched in 2025 or 2026 and promise easy money, be skeptical.

2. Firms that only pay in their own token. Legitimate firms pay in USDT or fiat. Tokens you cannot sell are not payment.

3. Unrealistic profit targets. If a firm claims you can make 50% in a week, it is a scam.

---

### Verdict

For crypto prop trading, stick with the same firms that are proven in forex. FundedNext and FTMO both offer crypto instruments with the same reliability. Do not chase crypto-native prop firms with no payout history.
